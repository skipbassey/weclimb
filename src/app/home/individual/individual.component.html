Individual Counseling

Self Exploration

Individual counseling involves talking about your feelings, thoughts, and behaviors and the things that are troubling you. I will work with you to set goals or identify the things you would like to accomplish by working together. My counseling model attempts to empower you with the resources needed to make positive changes in your life. The counselor and client work together to define and discuss personal issues and reach mutually agreed upon goals.
